Trust that real life is better than your fantasy

Do not get too attached to fantasies that live only in the mind. You can spin your wheels in hope. If you don’t take action that leads you in new directions, you will stay stuck. Sometimes we have to let go of something we have hoped for in order to receive something greater, more real. To bring your dreams to life, you must release the attachment to long-held fantasies. Real life wants to deliver something even better than you could imagine, but first you mustempty your cup, and let go of old self-doubt and hopelessness. What will come will be better than your wildest dreams..
